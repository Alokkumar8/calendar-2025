Public Holidays in Antigua and Barbuda in Calendar 2025

As we look ahead to the year 2025, understanding the key public holidays in the calendar 2025 is important for planning. Antigua and Barbuda observe a variety of public holidays throughout the year, and being aware of these dates can help you organize vacations and special events. Below is a detailed list of all the public holidays in Antigua and Barbuda for the year 2025.

1. New Year's Day - January 1, 2025

New Year's Day marks the start of the year and is celebrated with enthusiasm in Antigua and Barbuda. Families and friends come together for gatherings, parties, and fireworks to welcome the new year with joy and optimism.

2. Good Friday - April 18, 2025

Good Friday commemorates the crucifixion of Jesus Christ and is observed as a solemn day of reflection. Many people in Antigua and Barbuda attend church services and spend the day in prayer and meditation.

3. Easter Monday - April 21, 2025

Easter Monday is a day for celebration and relaxation following the Easter Sunday festivities. It is often marked by family outings, picnics, and community events across the islands.

4. Labour Day - May 5, 2025

Labour Day honors the contributions of workers in Antigua and Barbuda. The day typically features parades, speeches, and cultural activities, recognizing the importance of labor rights and achievements.

5. Whit Monday - June 9, 2025

Whit Monday is celebrated as a public holiday following Pentecost Sunday. It is a day of rest and celebration, often observed with religious services and community gatherings.

6. Carnival Monday - August 4, 2025

Carnival Monday is one of the most vibrant and colorful holidays in Antigua and Barbuda, marking the start of the annual Carnival festivities. The day is filled with music, dance, and parades, showcasing the islands' rich culture and heritage.

7. Carnival Tuesday - August 5, 2025

Carnival Tuesday continues the festivities with more grand parades, costumes, and celebrations. It is the climax of the Carnival season, drawing large crowds and tourists to the islands.

8. Independence Day - November 1, 2025

Independence Day celebrates Antigua and Barbuda's independence from British rule in 1981. The day is marked with national pride, featuring flag-raising ceremonies, cultural performances, and fireworks.

9. National Heroes Day - December 9, 2025

National Heroes Day honors the individuals who have made significant contributions to the nation’s history and development. It is a day of remembrance and celebration, with ceremonies and educational events held in their honor.

10. Christmas Day - December 25, 2025

Christmas Day is celebrated with great joy across Antigua and Barbuda. Families come together for festive meals, gift exchanges, and church services, reflecting the spirit of love and togetherness.

11. Boxing Day - December 26, 2025

Boxing Day is a public holiday celebrated the day after Christmas. It is a time for relaxation, socializing, and enjoying sports or community events with friends and family.
