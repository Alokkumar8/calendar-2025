Public Holidays in American Samoa in Calendar 2025

As we look ahead to the year 2025, understanding the key public holidays in the calendar 2025 is important for planning. American Samoa observes a variety of public holidays throughout the year, and being aware of these dates can help you organize vacations and special events. Below is a detailed list of all the public holidays in American Samoa for the year 2025.

1. New Year's Day - January 1, 2025

New Year's Day marks the beginning of the new year and is a time of celebration in American Samoa. Families and friends gather for feasts, parties, and fireworks to welcome the year with joy and optimism.

2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day - January 20, 2025

This federal holiday honors the legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. It is a day of reflection, service, and community events to celebrate his contributions to civil rights and social justice.

3. Presidents' Day - February 17, 2025

Presidents' Day is observed to honor the contributions of all U.S. presidents, particularly George Washington and Abraham Lincoln. It is celebrated with parades, educational programs, and community gatherings.

4. Good Friday - April 18, 2025

Good Friday is a significant day in American Samoa, observed with solemn church services and prayers to commemorate the crucifixion of Jesus Christ. Many residents participate in community worship and reflection.

5. Memorial Day - May 26, 2025

Memorial Day honors the sacrifices of military personnel who died in service to the United States. In American Samoa, the day is marked by ceremonies at cemeteries, parades, and community events.

6. Independence Day - July 4, 2025

Independence Day is a major celebration in American Samoa, featuring fireworks, parades, and patriotic festivities. It commemorates the signing of the Declaration of Independence in 1776.

7. Labor Day - September 1, 2025

Labor Day recognizes the contributions of workers to the development and prosperity of the nation. In American Samoa, the day is often observed with picnics, sports, and community activities.

8. White Sunday - October 12, 2025

White Sunday is a unique and significant holiday in American Samoa, dedicated to children. Families attend church services where children lead prayers and performances. The day is marked by feasts and celebrations honoring the youth.

9. Veterans Day - November 11, 2025

Veterans Day pays tribute to all military veterans for their service. It is observed with ceremonies, parades, and community events that honor the sacrifices of those who served in the armed forces.

10. Thanksgiving Day - November 27, 2025

Thanksgiving Day is a time for gratitude and family gatherings in American Samoa. Families come together to enjoy a traditional feast, including turkey and other local dishes, and reflect on their blessings.

11. Christmas Day - December 25, 2025

Christmas Day celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ and is one of the most cherished holidays in American Samoa. The day is marked with church services, festive meals, and gift exchanges, along with the decoration of homes and Christmas trees.
